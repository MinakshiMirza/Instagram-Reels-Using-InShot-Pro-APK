Step 2: Open InShot Pro and Start a New Project

Once you have installed InShot Pro, open the app. You will see a home screen with three options: Video, Photo, and Collage. Since we are making a Reel, tap on Video.

  • Tap on the Video button: This will take you to your phone’s gallery where you can select the video clips you want to use.
  • Select your clips: Choose the video clips that you want to include in your Reel. You can select multiple clips.
  • Start editing: After selecting your clips, tap on the checkmark to start editing.

Step 3: Trim and Cut Your Clips

Editing your video clips is the most important step. InShot Pro makes it easy to trim and cut your videos.

  • Trim your clips: Tap on the clip you want to trim. You will see handles on both ends of the clip. Drag these handles to cut out unwanted parts.
  • Split your clips: If you want to split a clip into two parts, move the playhead (the white vertical line) to the point where you want to split, then tap on the Split button.
  • Delete unwanted parts: After splitting, you can delete the unwanted parts by selecting them and tapping on the Delete button.

Step 4: Add Music to Your Reel

Music is a key element in making your Reel engaging. InShot Pro allows you to add music easily.

  • Tap on the Music button: This is located at the bottom of the screen.
  • Add a track: You can choose music from InShot’s library, or you can add your own music by tapping on My Music.
  • Adjust the music: After adding the music, you can adjust its length and volume. You can also set the start and end points of the music track.

Step 5: Apply Filters and Effects

Filters and effects can make your Reel look more professional and attractive.

  • Tap on the Filter button: This is located at the bottom of the screen.
  • Choose a filter: Scroll through the available filters and choose one that you like. Tap on the filter to apply it to your video.
  • Adjust the filter: You can adjust the intensity of the filter by using the slider.

Step 6: Add Text and Stickers

Text and stickers can add a fun element to your Reel and help convey your message more clearly.

  • Tap on the Text button: This is located at the bottom of the screen.
  • Add your text: Type in the text you want to add. You can change the font, color, and style of the text.
  • Position your text: Move the text to the desired position on the screen.
  • Add stickers: Tap on the Sticker button to add fun stickers to your video. You can choose from a variety of stickers available in the app.

Step 7: Adjust Video Speed

Adjusting the speed of your video can create interesting effects.

  • Tap on the Speed button: This is located at the bottom of the screen.
  • Adjust the speed: Use the slider to increase or decrease the speed of your video. You can create slow-motion or fast-motion effects.

Step 8: Save and Export Your Reel

Once you are happy with your edits, it’s time to save and export your Reel.

  • Tap on the Save button: This is located at the top right corner of the screen.
  • Choose the resolution: Select the resolution for your video. Higher resolution means better quality but larger file size.
  • Export your video: Tap on Save to export your video. It will be saved to your phone’s gallery.

Step 9: Upload Your Reel to Instagram

Now that your Reel is ready, it’s time to upload it to Instagram.

  • Open Instagram: Launch the Instagram app on your phone.
  • Tap on the Reels icon: This is located at the bottom of the screen.
  • Upload your video: Tap on the camera icon, then swipe up to choose your video from your gallery.
  • Add finishing touches: You can add text, stickers, and music within Instagram if you want.
  • Share your Reel: Once you are happy with your Reel, tap on Share to upload it.

  • Be creative: Don’t be afraid to try new things and experiment with different editing techniques.
  • Add captions: Adding captions can help your audience understand your message even if they are watching without sound.
  • Engage with your audience: Ask questions or encourage your viewers to comment and share your Reels.
